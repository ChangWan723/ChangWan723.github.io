Travel in Thailand

Phuket

My trip to Phuket. I went to Patong Beach, Similan Island, Cat Island and other places. And I did my first underwater dive on Emperor Island.

Bangkok

My trip to Bangkok. I went to the Grand Palace, the zoo, the Reclining Buddha Temple and other places. And watched Thai opera.

Travel in Japan

Osaka

My trip to Osaka. If Kyoto was the city of the courtly nobility and Tokyo the city of the samurai, then Osaka was the city of the merchant class.

Nara

My trip to Nara. It's a lively city and I love the deer here. I have never been to a city with so many animals, which makes the city like a huge park.

Kyoto

My trip to Kyoto. Kyoto is the epitome of old Japan: atmospheric temples, sublime gardens, and women in Kimono.

Tokyo

My trip to Tokyo. Yoking past and future, Tokyo dazzles with its traditional culture and passion for everything new.

Fuji

My trip to Fuji. Of all Japan's iconic images, Mt Fuji (3776m) is the real deal. That night I stayed in a local folk house and experienced the local customs.

Life in U.K.

Southampton

My life in Southampton. Southampton is a beautiful harbour city. I live here for my master's degree. I've made new friends from all over the world here.

Cambridge

My trip to Cambridge. Cambridge is a separate city with distinguished universities, a host of historic sites, and artistic and cultural attractions galore.

Eastbourne

My trip to Eastbourne. I went to Seven Sisters Cliffs near Eastbourne. It's a beautiful place. We were lucky with the weather that day. But long walks do get tiring.

Brockenhurst

My trip to Brockenhurst. There's a big new forest park near Brockenhurst. There's a lot of wildlife in the forest. I took a walk in the forest and enjoyed the beauty of nature.

Weymouth

My trip to Weymouth. This is a trip organised by the university. Famous British food for lunch: Fish＆Chips. But I must say British food really isn't very good. ：）

Life in China

Yuncheng

My life in Yuncheng. I was born and raised there. Many photos were taken nearly a decade ago. Although they are blurred, they are undoubtedly the most valuable treasure of my photos.

Xi'an

My life in Xi'an. I've been living in Xi'an for almost 8 years, and this is a collection of photos from my time in Xi'an between 2015 and 2018, mostly from my university days.

Xi'an

My life in Xi'an. I've been living in Xi'an for almost 8 years, and this is a collection of photos from my time in Xi'an between 2018 and 2023, mostly from my working days.

Shanghai

My trip to Shanghai. I have been to Shanghai twice. Shanghai: few cities in the world evoke so much history, excess, glamour, mystique and exotic promise in name alone.

Chongqing

My trip to Chongqing. One of the most important city in western China. Chongqing makes up for a lack of top-notch sights with fantastic food and charismatic geography.

Guanshan ranch

My trip to Guanshan ranch. I went here with my colleagues from Huawei. My colleagues and I rode horses and archery here together, and in the evening we had a bonfire party.

Chengdu

My trip to Chengdu. I also went here with my colleagues from Huawei. Chengdu is the hometown of giant pandas. We mainly visited the panda breeding base here.

Qingdao

My trip to Qingdao. Combining fresh sea air and dashing good looks, Qīngdǎo – the name means 'Green Island' in Chinese. It's a beautiful seaside city in Shandong province.
