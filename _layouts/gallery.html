Travel in Japan

Osaka

My trip to Osaka. If Kyoto was the city of the courtly nobility and Tokyo the city of the samurai, then Osaka was the city of the merchant class.

Nara

My trip to Nara. It's a lively city and I love the deer here. I have never been to a city with so many animals, which makes the city like a huge park.

Kyoto

My trip to Kyoto. Kyoto is the epitome of old Japan: atmospheric temples, sublime gardens, and woman in Kimono.

Tokyo

My trip to Tokyo. Yoking past and future, Tokyo dazzles with its traditional culture and passion for everything new.

Fuji

My trip to Fuji. Of all Japan's iconic images, Mt Fuji (3776m) is the real deal. That night I stayed in a local folk house and experienced the local customs.
